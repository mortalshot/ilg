Does your company need drop shipping?

Typically, after a customer places an order, the item is sent from manufacturer to merchant to customer, but drop shipping can eliminate one of those steps. Instead, after the customer places an order, it is passed along to the manufacturer, who can then ship the product direct to the buyer. This speeds the process and reduces the risk of damaged or lost shipments. It also gives your company the ability to expand rapidly, without having to increase warehouse capacity, office space or other requisite infrastructure. For eCommerce vendors, particularly small- and medium-sized businesses, this is an excellent approach for saving time and money.
